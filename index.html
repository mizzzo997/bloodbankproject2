How to Donate

  1. Check if you meet the eligibility criteria, such as age and health requirements.
  2. Find a donation center or event near you using our locator tool.
  3. Schedule an appointment to streamline your donation process.
  4. Arrive hydrated and prepared for a quick and safe donation.
  5. Receive a thank-you and follow-up on your donation’s impact.
